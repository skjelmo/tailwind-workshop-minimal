TLDR; Use breakpoint prefixes (sm:text-green-500, lg:text-indigo-600) to apply styles at different breakpoints

“This card looks awesome on mobile devices. Can you use breakpoint prefixes to fix the font-size, padding and background gradient on larger displays?”

Eivind Skjelmo
Developer

Breakpoints.

Tailwind exposes default utilties for applying styles at specific breakpoints:

Breakpoint prefix Style applied from
0px
sm 640px
md 768px
lg 1024px
xl 1280
2xl 1536px

Usage.

Just apply the breakpoint prefix before any utility you want to apply at a give breakpoint:

class="px-2 sm:px-4 md:px-6 2xl:px-12"

The style is applied until it is overrided by a style with on a larger breakpoint.
